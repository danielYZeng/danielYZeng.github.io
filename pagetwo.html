Transportation Interest

During my time in Europe, I experienced various forms of transportation in a mass. Bus, Train, Plane, Metro, and of course cars. The difference is that public transit is abundant in Europe and much more efficient. Higher frequency, cleaner, newer, less expensive (although higher taxes help subsidize this), faster.. there's much more I can say. Also, in regards to planes, fares are cheaper overall.
